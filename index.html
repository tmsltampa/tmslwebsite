Who We Are

More than a league.

TMSL is a competitive men's soccer league built by and for the Muslim community in the Tampa Bay area. What started as a handful of brothers organizing pickup games has grown into an organized, multi-team league with real standings, real rivalries, and real community behind it.

We play hard, we play fair, and we show up for each other. On the field and off it. Matches pause for prayer. Families come out to watch. New players are always welcome, whatever your skill level.
