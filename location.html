Parking

Free parking is available at the fields. Arrive 15 to 20 minutes before kickoff during doubleheader nights as the lot fills up fast.

Arrival Instructions

Turn into the road marked "Yuengling Brewery & Truck Entrance" on N 30th St. Head straight all the way down and turn left. You will see the soccer fields ahead.

Field Status & Weather

Florida storms move fast. Field status updates including delays and postponements are posted in the team WhatsApp groups by 4:00 PM on matchday. When in doubt, assume the match is on unless you hear otherwise from your captain.
