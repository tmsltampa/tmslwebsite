How It Works

Registration runs on a rolling basis each season. Submit the form below with your info and skill level — our team will place you with a squad based on need and your preferred position. If a season is full, you'll be added to the waitlist and contacted as soon as a spot opens up. There is no cost to register; team sponsorship covers field and league fees.
