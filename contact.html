FAQ

Common questions

How much does it cost to play?+

Registration is free for players. League costs are covered through team sponsorships and community support.

What skill level do I need?+

All levels are welcome, from first-timers to former club players. Teams are balanced to keep matches competitive and fun.

Do matches stop for prayer?+

Yes. TMSL respects prayer times, and matches accommodate breaks as needed, especially around Maghrib during evening games.

Can I bring my family to watch?+

Absolutely. TMSL matches are family-friendly community events — bring your kids, your parents, everyone.

How do I become a team captain?+

Reach out via the contact form below. Captain spots open each season based on team needs and prior involvement.

Is there a women's or youth league?+

Not yet, but we're exploring it as the league grows. Reach out if you're interested in helping get one started.
